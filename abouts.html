"Hello, organic food lovers!"

LeMorsels, IT'S NATURAL AND ORGANIC AS IT SHOULD BE!

Here in LeMorsels we are a research organization that wants more food products to be free of artificial food additives like artificial sweeteners, preservatives, coloring, flavoring, and monosodium glutamate (MSG). Some of our speakers surpervise if farmers are using organically grown crops tend to use natural fertilizers like manure to improve plant growth. The good of using organic grown crops is that animals raised organically don't need to much antibiotics or hormones. The most commonly purchased organic foods are fruits, vegetables, grains, dairy products and meat.
